Welcome to Dhruv Seva Trust
Welcome to DHRUV SEVA TRUST, a registered, non-commercial, and non-political organization dedicated to uplifting the lives of the deprived urban and rural masses living below the poverty line. Our mission is to extend a helping hand to the poorest of the poor, women, youth, children, disabled individuals, and those affected by disasters. At SEVA, we envision a world where marginalized individuals and communities are empowered, and their rights are safeguarded. As a trusted resource organization, Dhruv Seva Trust is actively engaged in various fields of social work. We work closely with grassroots groups, assisting them in organizing themselves and striving towards attaining equality, social justice, and freedom from exploitation. Our unwavering commitment lies in rendering services that address the immediate needs of the underprivileged while also focusing on sustainable long-term solutions. Together, we can make a significant impact and create a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and lead a dignified life.  

Seva and NGOs

It had involved in net work of voluntary organizations and consumer groups in Uttar Pradesh on consumer activism and training. It provides consultancy services for many NGOs in the state on result based management and ethical management of NGOs.
